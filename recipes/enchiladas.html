Cheese and Beef Enchiladas

Description

Want an alternative to Taco Tuesday? Warm up these delicious cheesy beef enchiladas. It is perfect for winter!

Ingredients

  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • 1 ½ cups diced red or green capcisum
  • 1 (1.25 oz) package taco seasoning
  • 8 (7 inch) flour or corn tortillas
  • 2 cups cheese
  • 1 ½ cups salsa or picante sauce
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup chopped tomato
  • ½ cup light sour cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat large skillet over medium-high heat; add meat and bell pepper.
  2. Cook 5 minutes, stirring until meat crumbles and is no longer pink; drain.
  3. Add taco seasoning mix and 1/2 cup water. Simmer, uncovered, 5 minutes or until sauce thickens, stirring occasionally.
  4. Spoon 1/3 cup meat mixture down center of each tortilla; sprinkle with 2 tablespoons cheese.
  5. Roll up; place seam-side down in greased 13x9-inch baking pan. Spoon salsa over enchiladas.
  6. Cover with foil; bake in preheated 375 degrees F oven 18 minutes or until heated through.
  7. Uncover; sprinkle with remaining cheese. Bake 2 minutes more or until cheese is melted.
  8. Serve with lettuce, tomato, and if desired, sour cream.
